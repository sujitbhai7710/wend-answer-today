Frequently Asked Questions

What is LinkedIn Wend?

LinkedIn Wend is a free daily word search puzzle from LinkedIn. Each day, a new grid invites you to trace hidden words through adjacent cells. The words wind through the grid, changing direction as they go — that’s how the game got its name, “Wend,” meaning to travel along a winding course.

How often are new Wend puzzles released?

New LinkedIn Wend puzzles drop daily at 1:30 PM IST (8:00 AM UTC). Our site updates automatically at 1:31 PM IST with the latest answers, so you always have the solution within one minute of the puzzle going live.

Are the answers on this site accurate?

Yes. Every answer is verified against the official LinkedIn Wend game before publishing. We use automated tools to extract puzzle data directly from the source, ensuring 100% accuracy for every word and cell position.

Is this site affiliated with LinkedIn?

No. This is an independent fan resource and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to LinkedIn or Microsoft. We simply provide answers and hints for the daily Wend puzzle to help players who are stuck.

How do I play LinkedIn Wend?

Tap or click the first letter of a word, then trace through adjacent cells (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) to spell it out. Short words (3–4 letters) are usually the easiest to spot first. Look for uncommon letter combos that hint at the start of a longer word — each word snakes through the grid, changing direction as it goes.

What time does the new Wend puzzle come out?

New puzzles release every day at 1:30 PM IST (8:00 AM UTC / 4:00 AM EDT). Our answers are typically posted by 1:31 PM IST — just one minute after the puzzle goes live.
