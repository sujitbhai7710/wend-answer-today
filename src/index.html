Frequently Asked Questions

What is LinkedIn Wend?

LinkedIn Wend is a free daily word search game from LinkedIn. Each day, a new puzzle invites you to connect letters in a grid and uncover hidden words. The words wind through the grid, changing direction as they go, which is why the game is called "Wend" — meaning to travel along a winding course.

How often are new Wend puzzles released?

New LinkedIn Wend puzzles are released daily at 1:30 PM IST (Indian Standard Time). Our site updates automatically at 1:31 PM IST with the latest answers and explanations, so you always have access to the solution right after the new puzzle goes live.

Are the answers on this site accurate?

Yes. Every answer is verified against the official LinkedIn Wend game before posting. We use automated tools to extract the puzzle data directly from the game, ensuring 100% accuracy for every word and cell position.

Is this site affiliated with LinkedIn?

No. We are an independent resource. This site is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to LinkedIn or Microsoft. We simply provide answers and hints for the Wend game to help players who are stuck.

How do I play LinkedIn Wend?

In Wend, you need to find hidden words in a letter grid. Each word winds through the grid, changing direction as it goes. Start by finding short words (3-4 letters) first, as they are usually easier to spot. Look for uncommon letter combinations that might signal the start of a longer word. The words always connect adjacent cells — horizontally, vertically, or they can change direction mid-word.

What time does the new Wend puzzle come out?

New LinkedIn Wend puzzles are released daily at 1:30 PM IST (Indian Standard Time), which is 8:00 AM UTC / 4:00 AM EDT. Our answers are typically posted by 1:31 PM IST each day, just one minute after the new puzzle becomes available.
